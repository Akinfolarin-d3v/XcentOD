About Artiste

Eccentric, fast-rising and talented Nigerian music act, Xcent OD hails from Utuh in Nnewi South Local Government Area of Anambra State. He attended Christ The King College, Onitsha and later proceeded to bag a B.Sc in Music from Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka, also in Anambra State. This promising young talent, born first in a family of three, Xcent OD derives his inspirations from diverse means. Creativity isn’t limited, according to him. Nothing makes more sense than singing, travelling, and sightseeing.
